Get To Know The Owner

With 10 years of residential framing experience, Liran "Lee" blends problem-solving skills and hands-on expertise. Holding a BA in Mass Communications from Carleton University, he shifted to framing after leaving the corporate software sales world. Lee quickly rose to manage crews and oversee projects, recognized for his ability to correct and optimize work. Dedicated to professionalism, knowledge sharing, and transparency, Lee is committed to building quality homes. Outside of work, he enjoys family time, fishing, reading, and cheering for the Dallas Cowboys.
